How to Test the Extension

New Feature: Hover over any highlighted hate speech to see both the detailed information tooltip AND an X button to remove the content!

  1. Load the extension in your browser
  2. Wait for the page to be scanned
  3. Look for highlighted text (red border with pulsing animation)
  4. Hover over highlighted text to see:
    • Informational tooltip showing confidence level and AI justification
    • Gray X button in the top-right corner
  5. Click the X to immediately remove the content (no confirmation needed)
  6. Removed content will be replaced with "[Content Removed]" text
